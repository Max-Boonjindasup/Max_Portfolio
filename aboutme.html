I graduated from the University of California, San Diego in 2018 with a double major in biochemistry and cognitive science (specialized in machine learning). I then joined Siemens, Amgen, and Cedars-Sinai to expand upon my research training and explore my interest in biological systems. It was during this time that I noticed a striking disparity between tech and non-tech companies where many valuable skills and tools failed to translate over. Wanting to bridge this gap, I created an automated system for analysis workflows, presented my project to the company, and led a team to develop it. This experience ignited my passion for merging healthcare and technology, supporting data-driven decisions to enhance patient care and tackle workforce shortages. Now, I aspire to be at the forefront of this transformative process and combine my problem-solving skills from research with data science to uncover insightful and impactful solutions.
